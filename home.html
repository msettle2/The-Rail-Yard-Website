    About the Garden

    Opening on March 17, 2023, The Elkin RailYard is an all outdoor beer garden which sits in the heart of the beautiful Yadkin Valley in Elkin, NC. The bar itself is located in one of our two graffitied shipping containers and consists of 16 taps featuring a wide variety of beer from not only NC but all over the world.
